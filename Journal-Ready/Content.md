# 🎨 PHASE 4: FINAL OPTIMIZATION

## 🎨 RESEARCH DIAGRAM TOOLKIT (ENHANCED RULES)

## 🔧 OBJECTIVE:
To generate publication-ready, humanized, journal-compliant figures and diagrams.

---

## 📊 DIAGRAM GENERATION RULES:

1. All diagrams MUST be:
   - Humanized (non-AI generic style)
   - Clean, professional, publication-ready

2. TEXT PLACEMENT RULE:
   - ALL text MUST be inside boxes/shapes
   - NO floating text outside diagram elements
   - Maintain clear readability (font size, spacing)

3. STRUCTURE RULE:
   - Logical flow (top-down or left-right)
   - Proper alignment and spacing
   - Consistent design across all figures

---

## 🧾 FIGURE-TEXT MAPPING (MANDATORY):

For EVERY figure generated:

1. Add in-text reference:
   Example:
   - "As shown in Figure 1, the study design follows..."

2. Clearly define:
   - What the figure represents
   - Why it is included

---

## 📍 FIGURE PLACEMENT RULE:

- Mention explicit placement instruction:
  
  Example:
  "Figure 1: Study Methodology Flowchart  
   (Insert after Methodology section, Paragraph 2)"

- Ensure:
  - Placement aligns with journal guidelines
  - Figures appear AFTER first citation in text

---

## 🖼️ FIGURE TYPES TO GENERATE:

- Methodology Flowchart
- Conceptual Framework
- Data Flow Diagram
- Graphical Abstract (if required)
- Statistical Visualization (if needed)

---

## 🔄 EXISTING FIGURE IMPROVEMENT:

- Redesign unclear figures

- Improve labeling and readability
