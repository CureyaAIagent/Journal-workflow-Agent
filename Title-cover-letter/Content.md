# 📄 TITLE PAGE GENERATION (JOURNAL-SPECIFIC)

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE:
Generate a fully compliant Title Page based on journal guidelines.

---

## 📋 TITLE PAGE MUST INCLUDE:

1. Full Title (optimized, concise, journal-appropriate)

2. Running Title (if required)

3. Author Details:
   - Full Names
   - Affiliations (Department, Institution, Country)
   - Email IDs
   - ORCID IDs (if available)

4. Corresponding Author:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone (if required)
   - Address

5. Keywords (as per journal limit)

6. Word Count (if required)

7. Conflict of Interest Statement

8. Funding Statement

9. Acknowledgment Section (if applicable)

---

## ⚙️ RULES:

- STRICT formatting as per journal
- Correct author order (DO NOT modify unless specified)
- Ensure completeness for submission systems

---

## 📤 OUTPUT:

📄 Journal-compliant Title Page (ready to upload)

# 📄 COVER LETTER GENERATION (JOURNAL-SPECIFIC)

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE:
Generate a persuasive, journal-aligned cover letter for submission.

---

## 📋 COVER LETTER STRUCTURE:

1. Editor Address:
   - Editor-in-Chief / Handling Editor
   - Journal Name

2. Opening Statement:
   - Submission intent
   - Manuscript title
   - Article type

3. Research Significance:
   - Novelty
   - Contribution to field
   - Relevance to journal scope

4. Journal Fit Justification:
   - Why this journal is suitable

5. Ethical Declarations:
   - No plagiarism
   - Not submitted elsewhere
   - Author approval confirmed

6. Optional:
   - Suggested reviewers (if required)

7. Closing:
   - Corresponding author details

---

## ⚙️ RULES:

- Professional academic tone
- Concise (250–400 words typically)
- Tailored to journal scope (NOT generic)

---

## 📤 OUTPUT:

📄 Journal-specific Cover Letter (submission ready)
